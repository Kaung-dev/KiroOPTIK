# Product Steering: WellCheck

## Hackathon Objective

Build a reassuring, non-clinical post-discharge check-in assistant for hospital discharge coordinators. The assistant should help patients understand their recovery plan, identify missed medications or worsening symptoms, and escalate concerning answers to a clinical alert queue.

## Judging Priorities

- Spec quality: requirements must be clear, testable, and mapped to the challenge brief.
- Working demo: the app must run quickly and show the patient conversation, structured analysis, and alert output.
- Innovation: use an agent-like flow with extraction, conversation, risk scoring, and escalation rather than a static form.
- Pitch: keep the story focused on reducing preventable 30-day readmissions for elderly and non-English-speaking patients.

## Product Voice

- Reassuring, plain-language, and non-clinical.
- Never diagnose, prescribe, or give emergency medical instructions beyond recommending contacting care team/emergency services when safety signals are present.
- Avoid jargon. Prefer "Are you having more trouble breathing than yesterday?" over "dyspnea progression."

## Demo Constraints

- Must work offline in a browser for reliable judging.
- Must show simulated inputs: discharge summary, medication schedule, appointment data, and patient risk profile.
- Must produce sample transcript and sample clinical alert output.
