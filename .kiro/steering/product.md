# Product Steering

Build WellCheck as a discharge coordination command center, not a general medical chatbot.

The winning story:

- Coordinators manage too many post-discharge patients manually.
- Patients misunderstand medication schedules, warning signs, and appointments.
- WellCheck converts discharge instructions into a structured check-in and an explainable outreach queue.

The assistant must sound reassuring, plain-language, and non-clinical. It may escalate concerns, but it must not diagnose or prescribe.
